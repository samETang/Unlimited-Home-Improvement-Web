Our Working Process

  1. Upon discussion of your remodelation needs we will set a date to meet in person.
  2. Once we meet in person we wil discuss your budget, your needs, and what we can do for you.
  3. If at this point you decide we are a right fit for eachother, a contract will be drawn up with our agreed terms. The contract will include the work to be performed, the cost of the work broken down by detailed sections, and a payment schedule that fits your needs.
  4. Once the contract is signed we will agree upon a start date and get straight to work.
  5. An open line of communication wil be kept throughout the entirety of your project.
