Who We Are

Our company is located in Staten Island, New York. We specialize in home remodelation and home improvement projects. We have over 20 years of experience in the field, working on both inside and outside houses and corporate buildings. We put all of our prospective employees through rigorous training to ensure our clients only recieve the best work.

Our Working Process

  1. Upon discussion of your remodelation needs we will set a date to meet in person.
  2. Once we meet in person we wil discuss your budget, your needs, and what we can do for you.
  3. If at this point you decide we are a right fit for eachother, we will draw up a contract based on our discussion.
  4. The contract will include the work to be performed, the cost of the work broken down by detailed sections, and a payment schedule that fits your needs.
  5. Once the contract is drawn up we will agree upon a start date and get straight to work!
